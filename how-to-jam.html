What kind of games can I make?

We can break games in to the follow broad categories:

  • Digital or Video Games
  • Table Top Games (boardgames, cardgames)
  • Alternative Games (Games that cross media and even into real life)

Digital Games

Following are some free tools you where you can make games in your browser or on your laptop/pc.

Twine

Twine is an open-source tool for telling interactive, nonlinear stories.

You don't need to write any code to create a simple story with Twine, but you can extend your stories with variables, conditional logic, images, CSS, and JavaScript when you're ready.

Twine publishes directly to HTML, so you can post your work nearly anywhere. Anything you create with it is completely free to use any way you like, including for commercial purposes.
